**Historical Average**

- Create a control chart for the metric.
- Decide on the baseline data.  This may be the entire range or the latter portion if a stable process has been identified.
- Use the average as the 75% credit point (baseline) – center of C range
- Assign the control limits to the 50% and 100% range for scoring purposes

**Modified Historical Average**

- Create a control chart for the metric.
- Decide on the baseline data.  This may be the entire range or the latter portion if a stable process has been identified.
- Use the average as the 75% credit point (baseline) – center of C range
- Adjust Upper and Lower Control Limits to current SLA, MASL and/or Industry Data
- Assign the control limits to the 50% and 100% range for scoring purposes

## Slide 19: 19

![Slide 19](limits-and-scoring/slide-019.png)

**Re-Baselining**

- Annual contract review
- Event Based Triggers
- Single point outside 3-sigma limit
- At lease 2 out of 3 successive values on same side of and more than 2 sigma units away from center line
- At least 4 out of 5 successive values on same side of and more than 1 sigma unit away from center line
- At least 8 successive values on same side of center line
- Western Electric Handbook
- Seismic Event
- Proactive, agree ahead of time on what to do (suspend scoring for 3 months and watch for example).
