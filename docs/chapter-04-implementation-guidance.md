# Chapter 4: Implementation Guidance

!!! info "Coming soon"
    Chapter 4 is being converted from the source drafts. The published version will cover what a practitioner needs to know to implement BSCP — the prerequisites, the go/no-go criteria (including the seniority of the participating client representatives), the typical timeline (about 15 contact days spread over four to six months), the management of the homework-between-meetings rhythm, and the failure modes that are easiest to fall into.

It will also include a "Letter to My Younger Self" section — what I wish I had known when I was starting out, and what to watch for in your own first BSCP engagement. See [Contributing](about/contributing.md) for how to help.
