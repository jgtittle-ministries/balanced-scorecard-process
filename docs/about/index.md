# About this book

The Balanced Scorecard Process is a methodology developed by John G. Tittle for surfacing, measuring, and balancing the competing values an organization is actually operating under. This book documents the methodology, its origins, the cases that shaped it, and how to use it in your own work.

- **[How this book came to be](how-this-book-came-to-be.md)** — the origin story; where the methodology came from and why it took the form it did.
- **[Contributing](contributing.md)** — how readers can suggest edits, file issues, or open pull requests.

## Author

John G. Tittle — JGT Transformation Engineering. Author of [*Intentional Journey of the Heart*](https://jgtittle-ministries.github.io/Intentional-Journey-of-the-Heart/) and of this book; engineer and consultant; former Group leader at a large IT-services firm where BSCP was developed and applied across multiple client engagements over many years.

## Companion project

This book's held-tensions and competing-values structural insight is the same insight applied in a different register in the author's *Intentional Journey of the Heart* series — specifically the Vol 1 supplemental on "[Reading the Laws as Held Tensions](https://jgtittle-ministries.github.io/Intentional-Journey-of-the-Heart/volume-1-laws-of-the-spirit/supplemental-held-tensions-and-shadow-pairs/)." The two books are independent but cross-reference each other where the structural parallel is useful.
